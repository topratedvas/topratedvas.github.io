Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20 · v1 draft

Payments are simulated in this version — no real charges are made yet. The terms below describe how refunds will work once live checkout is enabled, and should be reviewed by a qualified attorney.

Employer unlocks & credits

  • 7-day response guarantee. If a VA you unlocked doesn't respond within 7 days, you automatically receive a free replacement credit — no request needed.
  • Unlocks are generally non-refundable once contact information has been revealed, because the value (the contact details) has been delivered. The response guarantee above is your protection against a non-responsive VA.
  • Unused credits expire 12 months after purchase and are non-refundable, but any contacts you've already unlocked remain yours forever.
  • Duplicate or clearly erroneous charges will be refunded.

VA exam fees

  • Exam fees are non-refundable once an exam has been started, since the exam content and grading are delivered on attempt.
  • If you paid but never started an exam, contact us within 14 days for a refund.
  • Failing an exam is not grounds for a refund — you receive one free retake after a 7-day wait, then $10 per additional retake, with your latest score counting.

VA listing fees

  • Your first passed exam includes 2 months of listing at no extra cost.
  • Monthly listing renewals ($5/mo) are non-refundable for the current period but you can cancel anytime; your listing simply becomes hidden at period end (scores and videos are kept).

How to request a refund

Email topratedvas@gmail.com from your account email with the details. Eligible refunds are issued to the original payment method.
