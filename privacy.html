Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20 · v1 draft

Draft notice: This is a v1 draft for a demonstration build and is not legal advice. Review with a qualified attorney before collecting real user data.

What we collect

  • Account data: email, role (VA or employer), and login activity.
  • VA profile data: name, country/timezone, certifications and scores, proof-video links, rates and availability, and contact details (WhatsApp/Telegram, email, external profile links).
  • Employer data: unlock history, credits, private notes, survey responses, and reviews.
  • Technical data: device/login information used for security (new-device alerts, geo-anomaly checks).

How contact info is protected

A VA's ghosted contact information (full name, email, WhatsApp/Telegram, external links) is stored in a protected area and is not readable before an unlock. Access is enforced by server-side security rules that check for a valid unlock record — not merely hidden in the page. Pay ranges remain visible so no one pays to discover a rate.

How we use data

  • To operate the marketplace: display profiles, process unlocks, and maintain dashboards.
  • To keep accounts secure: single-session enforcement, login alerts, and fraud checks.
  • To communicate: renewal reminders, availability heartbeats, and check-in surveys.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. A VA's contact details are shared only with an employer who has unlocked that VA. We use infrastructure providers (for example, hosting and authentication/database services) to run the platform.

Your choices

  • Update or remove your profile information at any time from your dashboard.
  • Request account deletion by contacting us.
  • A lapsed VA listing is hidden, not deleted; you may request full deletion.

Cookies

We use only the storage necessary to keep you signed in and remember basic preferences (such as language). We do not run third-party advertising trackers.
