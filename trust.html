Trust & Fairness

A certification only means something if it's hard to fake. Here's exactly how we keep ours honest.

Our promise

Every VA listed on TopRatedVAs has paid for and passed a role-specific certification exam, posted the required proof videos, and been approved by our review process. Employers browse everything free and pay only to unlock contact info — so our incentive is to keep the badges trustworthy, not to inflate them.

Test integrity

  • Randomized exams. Each attempt draws a random 20 questions from a larger bank per certification, and both the question order and the answer order are shuffled — no two VAs take the same test.
  • Scenario-based, not trivia. Questions are real situations — angry-buyer messages, repricing calls, VERO traps — that reward judgment, not memorization.
  • Answer speed is scored. Exams are untimed, but quick answers earn full marks, ~1 minute is fine, and long delays progressively reduce points. You may pause between questions; the rules are shown up front.
  • Typed answers only. Copy/paste is disabled, AI-generated-answer detection is active, and tab-switch or paste events are logged as flags for the reviewer.
  • Retakes. One free retake after a 7-day wait, then $10 each. Your latest score is the one that counts.

Proof videos (identity-bound)

  • Assigned work video. After you pass, the site generates a personal checklist ("show X, then Y, say today's date and your test ID out loud"). It can't be borrowed or pre-recorded.
  • Summary video. You explain the whole EcomSniper model on camera and state your name, the date, and "for my TopRatedVAs certification."

AI + human review

Exams are auto-graded instantly. Videos are reviewed against a rubric — were all checklist items shown, was the model explained correctly, and an English-clarity score (1–10) that appears on your profile. High-confidence passes are approved automatically; borderline cases go to a human queue, and every VA gets one human appeal.

In this early version, AI review verdicts are simulated while we wire up the live pipeline. The review UI and rules are real; the automated verdicts are placeholders.

Anti-cheating penalties

  • Confirmed cheating on an exam = 1-year suspension.
  • A second offense = lifetime ban, and a banned account can't re-register.
  • Account sharing is prohibited; one active login per account is enforced on both sides.

Guarantees for employers

  • 7-day response guarantee. If a VA you unlocked doesn't respond within 7 days, you automatically receive a free replacement credit.
  • Verified-contact reviews only. Only employers who actually unlocked a VA can review them — no anonymous or fake ratings.
  • Unlocked contacts are yours forever, saved in your dashboard even after credits expire.

Independence

TopRatedVAs is an independent training & marketplace platform and is not affiliated with EcomSniper. We're a certification and directory service — not a staffing agency — and we never touch salaries. VAs are independent professionals; employers hire and pay them directly.
