Frequently asked questions

Everything about hiring, getting certified, unlocks, and guarantees. Still stuck? The chat bubble (bottom-right) can help.

For employers (dropshippers)

How much does it cost to browse?

Browsing is free, forever — every profile shows test scores, AI-scored English, proof videos, and verified reviews. You pay only when you want to reveal a VA's contact info.

What does an unlock include?

An unlock reveals the VA's full name, WhatsApp/Telegram, email, résumé, and profile links (OnlineJobs.ph, Upwork, etc.). It's $29 for a single unlock, or credit packs: 5 for $79 ($15.80 each) or 15 for $199 (~$13.27 each). Unlocked contacts stay in your dashboard forever, even after credits expire.

What if the VA never responds?

Our 7-day response guarantee: if an unlocked VA doesn't reply within 7 days, you automatically get a free replacement credit.

Can I earn free unlocks?

Yes. Over the first month after an unlock, four quick check-in surveys each earn ¼ of a credit — a full month of feedback earns your next unlock free.

Do you take a cut of the VA's pay?

No. We're a certification + directory platform, not a staffing agency. You hire and pay the VA directly — no middleman and no salary markup.

For virtual assistants

How do I get certified?

Study the free course, pay for an exam ($15 for your first, with discounts as you add more), pass a randomized scenario exam, record your two proof videos, and get approved by review. Scoring: 80+ = Certified, 90+ = Pro, 95+ = Elite.

What does it cost, and what do I get?

Exams are $15 / $15 / $10 / $10 (or a $45 bundle for all four), and the VA Manager exam is free after you pass all four. Your first pass puts your profile live for 2 months free, then it's $5/month to stay listed.

What happens if my listing lapses?

It's hidden, not deleted — your scores, videos, and certifications are kept forever. Reactivate anytime for $5. Every 30 days you confirm "I'm still available" via an email link so employers see fresh availability.

Can I retake an exam?

Yes — one free retake after a 7-day wait, then $10 each. Your latest score is the one that counts.

Trust & safety

How do you stop cheating?

Randomized questions and answer order, speed-scored answers, disabled copy/paste, AI-answer detection, and identity-bound proof videos. Cheating means a 1-year suspension; a second offense is a lifetime ban. See our Trust & Fairness page.

Are the reviews real?

Only employers who actually unlocked a VA can leave a review — no anonymous or fake ratings.

Is this affiliated with EcomSniper?

No. TopRatedVAs is an independent training & marketplace site. EcomSniper is a product of its respective owner.
